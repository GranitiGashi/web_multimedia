A new way to design

Home furniture design merges style with practicality, creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional. Thoughtful selection and arrangement of pieces reflect personal taste and enhance comfort, transforming any living area into an inviting and harmonious environment.

Elements

Room elements such as lighting, color, and furniture play a crucial role in defining a space's atmosphere. Carefully chosen accents and textures add depth and character, while functional pieces ensure comfort and usability. Together, these elements create a balanced and cohesive environment that reflects personal style.

Colors and Materials

When you change colors and materials, you can instantly change your design's entire look and feel. Select an object and change its color, or drag and drop textures and materials onto the object to add more depth

Precision tool

Draw precise straight lines, arcs, and circles with the precision tool. It's great for architectural or mechanical drawings. Drag your finger or Apple Pencil to create a precise drawing — uMake will recognize your hand movements and create the desired shape.
